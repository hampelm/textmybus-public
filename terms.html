  1. When you send a message to TextMyBus, we will respond with text messages to provide the information you requested. Once you recieve that information, we will not send you additional messages unless you request additional information.

  2. If you want to stop recieving texts from TextMyBus, reply to any message with STOP. To continue using the service after sending STOP, just send a new text to 50464.
  3. If you need help using the service, just text HELP to 50464. We will reply with basic instructions on how to use TextMyBus.

  6. For all questions about the services provided by this service and short code, please email detroit@codeforamerica.org.

Privacy Policy

Information collection and use

When you send a text message to TextMyBus, we recieve your phone number. We use your phone number to provide you with the bus data you requested. We do not store your phone number or share it with anyone.

We use internal logging tools and KISSMetrics to record information ("Log Data") created by your use of TextMyBus. Log Data may include the bus stop and route requested, time and date of the request, and other search terms. This information is not associated with your identity and is used only to understand overall usage patterns and improve the TextMyBus service. It is never sold or provided to third parties.
